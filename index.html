Keep it Simple Security Awareness & Training

This website provides links to FREE training courses and other training materials related to information security awareness. The best approach is an information security awareness program that includes policies, training, assessment, and monitoring. If you aren't ready for all that yet, this site will help you find ways to get started at improving your information security status. Explore this site to help you formulate your plan and find the materials you need to meet your training objectives. The materials referred to, and linked to, are free (or were when they were found)
